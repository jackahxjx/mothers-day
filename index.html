A letter for you

From Jack

Dear Mom,

Thank you for every meal you cook that I probably don't thank you for enough. For every time you ask how my day was and actually listen to the answer. Even when I just say "fine."

I know I can be a lot. The messy room, the attitude, the "I'm fine" when I'm clearly not. But you never stop trying. You never stop caring. Even when I make it hard, you keep showing up. That doesn't go unnoticed. I see it, even if I don't act like it.

You've given up so much so I could have what I have. You worry about me way more than you let on. And you love me in a never-ending way that I honestly don't think I'll ever fully understand.

One day I'll move out, and you'll miss having me around, or at least miss having to ask somone to fill your ice cup. But even then, nothing changes between us. You'll always be my person. You'll always be my mom.

Happy Mother's Day. I love you more than you'll ever know, even if it looks like i don't try.

With all my love,

Jack ❤️
